Got bored ?

If so, then stop here and have a break playing our simple but pretty game : Tic-Tac-Toe.

RULES:

X always goes first. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the board until either  one player has three in a row, horizontally, vertically or diagonally; or all nine squares are filled. If a player is able to draw three Xs or three Os in a row, that player wins. If all nine squares are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game is a draw
